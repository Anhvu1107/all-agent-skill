# Platform Routing

Use this before doing ASO work.

## Route By Store

- Apple App Store: prioritize title, subtitle, keyword field, promo text, and screenshot sequencing
- Google Play: prioritize title, short description, long description, and conversion assets
- both stores: separate what is shared from what must be customized per platform

## Route By Job

- research and positioning
- keyword and metadata optimization
- creative asset review
- launch or iteration planning
