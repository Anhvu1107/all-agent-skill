# Deck Quality Bar

Use this before calling a PPTX complete.

## Required Checks

- every slide has a clear role in the narrative
- typography, spacing, and alignment stay consistent across slides
- no content falls outside the slide bounds
- theme colors and accent patterns feel intentional rather than random
- exported thumbnails or rendered previews match expectations

## Common Failure Modes

- editing one slide but leaving the rest stylistically mismatched
- adding too much text instead of designing the slide
- breaking masters, placeholders, or speaker notes during manual edits
